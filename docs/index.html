About Baskit

Baskit is a privacy-focused collaborative shopping list app that lets you create, share, and manage shopping lists with friends and family. Start using it immediately without registration, or sign in to sync across devices and collaborate in real-time.

Key Features:

  • Guest-first experience (no registration required)
  • Real-time collaboration when signed in
  • Offline support with automatic sync
  • Cross-platform (iOS, Android, Web)
  • Privacy-focused design
