1. Introduction

Welcome to Baskit! This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our collaborative shopping list application ("Baskit," "we," "us," or "our").

Privacy-First Design: Baskit is designed with privacy in mind. You can use the app without creating an account, and all data is stored locally on your device until you choose to sign in for collaboration features.

By using Baskit, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide Directly

Data Type Purpose Storage Location
Shopping Lists Core app functionality - creating and managing your shopping lists Device (local) / Firebase (when signed in)
Shopping Items Adding items to your lists with names, quantities, and completion status Device (local) / Firebase (when signed in)
List Sharing Information Email addresses of people you share lists with Firebase (when using collaboration features)

2.2 Authentication Information (Optional)

When you choose to sign in to enable collaboration features:

  • Google Account Information: Email address, display name, profile picture (when using Google Sign-In)
  • Firebase Authentication ID: Unique identifier for your account
  • Anonymous Authentication: Temporary anonymous ID (automatically created, no personal information)

2.3 Technical Information

  • Device Information: App version, platform (Android/iOS), basic device specifications
  • Usage Analytics: App crashes and performance data (anonymized)
  • Local Storage: App preferences and settings stored on your device
Important: If you use Baskit without signing in, all your data remains exclusively on your device. We do not collect or have access to any of your shopping lists or personal information in guest mode.

3. How We Use Your Information

3.1 Core Functionality

  • Creating, editing, and managing your shopping lists
  • Syncing your lists across your devices (when signed in)
  • Enabling real-time collaboration on shared lists
  • Providing offline access to your lists

3.2 Communication and Collaboration

  • Sending list invitations to people you choose to collaborate with
  • Notifying collaborators of list updates (when enabled)
  • Managing list sharing permissions and access

3.3 App Improvement

  • Analyzing app performance and fixing technical issues
  • Understanding feature usage to improve the user experience
  • Ensuring app security and preventing abuse
No Advertising: We do not use your personal information for advertising purposes, nor do we sell your data to third parties.

4. Data Storage and Security

4.1 Storage Locations

  • Local Storage: All data is initially stored on your device using secure local storage
  • Firebase/Google Cloud: When you sign in, data is securely stored in Google's Firebase platform
  • Geographic Location: Cloud data is stored in secure data centers primarily in the United States

4.2 Security Measures

  • All data transmission is encrypted using industry-standard TLS encryption
  • Firebase security rules ensure only authorized users can access shared lists
  • Your device data is protected by your device's built-in security features
  • Regular security updates and monitoring

4.3 Data Migration

When you sign in for the first time, any existing local lists are automatically and securely migrated to your cloud account, ensuring no data loss while enabling collaboration features.

5. Sharing Your Information

5.1 User-Initiated Sharing

We only share your information when you explicitly choose to:

  • Share shopping lists with specific people (they see list content and your display name)
  • Collaborate on lists (collaborators see your contributions and basic profile info)

5.2 Service Providers

We use trusted third-party services that may process your data:

  • Google Firebase: For authentication, database, and hosting services
  • Google Sign-In: For optional account authentication

5.3 Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information if required by law, such as:

  • Complying with legal processes or government requests
  • Protecting our rights, property, or safety
  • Preventing fraud or abuse of our services
No Data Selling: We never sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties for marketing or commercial purposes.

6. Third-Party Services

6.1 Firebase Services (Google)

  • Firebase Authentication: Manages user sign-in and account security
  • Cloud Firestore: Stores and syncs your shopping lists across devices
  • Firebase Analytics: Provides anonymized app usage insights

Firebase services are governed by Google's Privacy Policy.

6.2 Google Sign-In

When you choose to sign in with Google, Google's privacy policy applies to the authentication process. We only receive basic profile information that you consent to share.

6.3 Third-Party Links

Our app may contain links to external websites or services. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these third-party sites.

7. Data Retention

7.1 Account Data

  • Active Accounts: Data is retained as long as your account is active
  • Inactive Accounts: Data may be deleted after 3 years of inactivity
  • Deleted Accounts: Data is permanently deleted within 30 days of account deletion

7.2 Local Data

  • Local data remains on your device until you delete the app or clear app data
  • When you sign out, local cloud-synced data is removed from your device

7.3 Shared Lists

  • Lists you've shared remain accessible to collaborators even if you delete your account
  • Your contributions to shared lists may remain visible to other collaborators

8. Your Rights and Choices

8.1 Access and Control

  • View Your Data: All your data is visible within the app
  • Edit Information: Update your profile, lists, and items at any time
  • Export Data: Request a copy of your data by contacting us
  • Delete Data: Delete individual lists, items, or your entire account

8.2 Privacy Controls

  • Guest Mode: Use the app without any cloud data collection
  • Selective Sharing: Choose exactly which lists to share and with whom
  • Revoke Access: Remove collaborators from your lists at any time
  • Sign Out: Remove all cloud data from your device while keeping your account active

9. Children's Privacy

Baskit is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately, and we will take steps to remove such information.

For users between 13 and 18, we recommend parental guidance when using collaboration features that involve sharing information with others.

10. International Users

Baskit is available globally, but our primary data storage is located in the United States. By using our service, you consent to the transfer of your information to the United States and other countries where our service providers operate.

We take appropriate safeguards to ensure your privacy rights are protected in accordance with applicable laws, including GDPR for European Union users.

10.1 GDPR Rights (EU Users)

If you are located in the European Union, you have additional rights under GDPR:

  • Right to access your personal data
  • Right to rectification of inaccurate data
  • Right to erasure ("right to be forgotten")
  • Right to restrict processing
  • Right to data portability
  • Right to object to processing

11. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or applicable laws. When we make significant changes, we will:

  • Update the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy
  • Notify users through the app or via email (if you have an account)
  • Provide at least 30 days notice for material changes

Your continued use of Baskit after changes take effect constitutes acceptance of the updated Privacy Policy.
